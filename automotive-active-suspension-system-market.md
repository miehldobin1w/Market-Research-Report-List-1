<p><p>The automotive active suspension system market consists of various types: </p><p>1. **Air Suspension** uses compressed air to adjust ride height and absorb shocks for enhanced comfort.</p><p>2. **Hydraulic Suspension** relies on hydraulic fluids to adapt suspension settings dynamically, improving stability and handling.</p><p>3. **Electromagnetic Suspension** employs electromagnetic forces to control wheel dynamics, providing precise adjustments for optimal ride quality.</p><p>4. **Electro-hydraulic Suspension** combines electric and hydraulic systems, enabling responsive adjustments for improved driving performance and comfort across varying conditions.</p></p>
