Coarse Wools, like those from sheep breeds such as Dorset, offer durability and warmth, making them ideal for outerwear. Medium Wools, such as Merino, strike a balance between softness and resilience, suitable for a variety of projects. Fine Wools, like cashmere and alpaca, provide ultimate softness and drape, primarily used in luxury garments and accessories.</p></p>
