<p><p>Machine Direction Orienters (MDOs) are classified into horizontal and vertical types based on their configuration and operational efficiency in the production of oriented films. Horizontal MDOs align materials in a flat position, facilitating continuous processing, while vertical MDOs orient films from above, allowing for space-saving and enhanced throughput. Both types play crucial roles in producing high-quality, stretchable films used in packaging and other applications, catering to varying production requirements and industry standards.</p></p>
