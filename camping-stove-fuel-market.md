<p><p>Camping stove fuels can be categorized into three main types: gas, liquid, and solid fuels. Gas fuels, such as propane and butane, are popular for their convenience and ease of use. Liquid fuels, including white gas and kerosene, offer versatility but may require more careful handling. Solid fuels, like biomass or fuel tablets, are lightweight and easy to store, though they may produce ash and require longer cooking times. Each type has its unique advantages, catering to diverse camping needs.</p></p>
