<p><p>The Smart Label Materials market comprises two main types: solvent-based and solvent-free/water-based products. Solvent-based materials use organic solvents for adhesion, providing high performance for labels but often raising environmental concerns due to volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, solvent-free or water-based products utilize eco-friendly formulations with lower environmental impact, making them safer and more sustainable options. Both types cater to diverse applications, with industries prioritizing durability, flexibility, and compliance with regulatory standards in their label solutions.</p></p>
