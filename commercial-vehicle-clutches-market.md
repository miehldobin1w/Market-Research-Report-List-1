<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Clutches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Friction Clutches</li><li>Electromagnetic Clutches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Clutches market includes primarily two types: friction clutches and electromagnetic clutches. Friction clutches operate using friction materials to engage or disengage the engine's power, ensuring smooth gear shifts and efficient torque transfer. Electromagnetic clutches, on the other hand, utilize an electromagnetic field to connect or disconnect the engine's input and output, allowing for precise control and rapid engagement. Both types are critical for optimizing performance and enhancing the drivability of commercial vehicles.</p></p>
