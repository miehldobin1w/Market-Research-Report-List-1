<p><p>The bicycle brakes market comprises three main types: disc brakes, rim brakes, and drum brakes. Disc brakes offer superior stopping power and heat dissipation, making them popular for mountain and high-performance bikes. Rim brakes are lighter and simpler in construction, providing adequate braking for road and casual bikes but are affected by weather conditions. Drum brakes are enclosed systems, offering reliable performance and consistent braking in various conditions, primarily used in commuter and utility bicycles. Each type caters to specific cycling needs.</p></p>
