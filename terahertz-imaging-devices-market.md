<p><p>The Terahertz Imaging Devices market consists of two primary types: passive terahertz imaging and active terahertz imaging. Passive terahertz imaging detects natural terahertz radiation emitted by objects, making it suitable for applications like surveillance and building inspections. In contrast, active terahertz imaging involves the generation and detection of terahertz waves, allowing for detailed material characterization and inspection processes. Each type has unique applications, contributing to advancements in fields such as security, healthcare, and materials science.</p></p>
