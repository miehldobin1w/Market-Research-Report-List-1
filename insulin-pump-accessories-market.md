<p><p>The insulin pump accessories market comprises various types essential for effective diabetes management. Tubing facilitates the flow of insulin from the pump to the body. Reservoirs hold the insulin within the pump, ensuring a steady supply. Infusion sets connect the tubing to the skin, delivering insulin directly. Other accessories may include adhesive patches, battery packs, and carrying cases that enhance the pump's usability and comfort. These components collectively contribute to the overall functionality and user experience of insulin pumps.</p></p>
