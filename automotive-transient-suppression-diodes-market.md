In contrast, bi-polar diodes can conduct current in both directions, offering versatility for protecting sensitive components from reverse voltage transients. Both types play crucial roles in safeguarding automotive electronics, ensuring reliability and performance under various voltage conditions, particularly in modern vehicles with complex electronic systems.</p></p>
