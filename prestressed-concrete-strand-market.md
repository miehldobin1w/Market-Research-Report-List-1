<p><p>The prestressed concrete strand market can be segmented into several types. Bare PC strand is used mainly in standard applications where no additional protection is needed. Grease-filled PC strand has a lubricant injected to prevent corrosion and enhance durability, making it suitable for harsher environments. Wax-filled PC strand offers a moisture barrier, increasing protection against environmental damage while maintaining flexibility.
