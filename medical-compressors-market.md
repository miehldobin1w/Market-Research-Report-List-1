<p><p>The medical compressors market is primarily categorized into vertical and horizontal compressors, each with distinct applications. Vertical compressors are designed to save space and are often used in environments where height is not a constraint, making them suitable for smaller medical facilities. In contrast, horizontal compressors typically offer higher airflow rates and are used in larger medical institutions or for applications requiring consistent and high-volume output. Both types are crucial for providing reliable gas supply in medical settings.</p></p>
