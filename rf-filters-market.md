<p><strong>The RF Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SAW</li><li>BAW</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF filters are essential components in wireless communication systems, ensuring signal integrity by allowing specific frequency ranges to pass while blocking others. The Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) filters are known for their compact size and efficiency, primarily used in mobile devices and consumer electronics. In contrast, Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) filters, which operate at higher frequencies, offer superior performance in terms of bandwidth and temperature stability, making them ideal for telecom infrastructure and military applications. Both types play crucial roles in modern communication technologies.</p></p>
