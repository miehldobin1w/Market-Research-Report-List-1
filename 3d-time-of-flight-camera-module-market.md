<p><p>The 3D Time of Flight (ToF) Camera Module market can be segmented into direct and indirect types. Direct ToF cameras measure the time it takes for light signals to bounce back from objects, providing accurate depth information in real-time. Indirect ToF cameras, on the other hand, utilize a phase shift technique, capturing depth data by analyzing light patterns from a modulated source. Each type caters to different applications, with direct modules often favored for real-time analysis and indirect modules suitable for detailed imaging tasks.</p></p>
