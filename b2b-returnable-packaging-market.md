<p><p>The B2B returnable packaging market includes various types, primarily Intermediate Bulk Containers (IBCs) and Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (FIBCs). IBCs are robust, reusable containers designed for transporting liquids and bulk materials, typically made from rigid materials like plastic or metal. FIBCs, or bulk bags, are lightweight, flexible containers made of woven fabric, ideal for dry bulk products. Both types enhance sustainability by reducing waste, lowering shipping costs, and improving inventory management in supply chains across various industries.</p></p>
