<p><p>The carbon and graphite felt market comprises three main types: pitch-based felt, PAN (polyacrylonitrile) based felt, and rayon-based felt. Pitch-based felt is known for its high thermal conductivity and is often used in high-temperature applications. PAN-based felt offers excellent mechanical properties and is widely utilized in aerospace and energy sectors. Rayon-based felt, while less common, provides a balance of properties suitable for various industrial uses. Each type serves specific thermal and electrical insulation needs across diverse industries.</p></p>
