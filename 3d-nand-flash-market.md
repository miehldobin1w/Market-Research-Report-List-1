<p><p>The 3D NAND Flash market is categorized into several types based on cell technology. Multi-Level Cell (MLC) stores two bits per cell, offering a balance between performance and cost, suitable for consumer applications. Trinary-Level Cell (TLC) stores three bits per cell, enhancing storage density and reducing costs, ideal for smartphones and SSDs. Other types include Quad-Level Cell (QLC) and SLC (Single-Level Cell), each targeting specific applications and performance requirements, catering to various market demands in storage solutions.</p></p>
