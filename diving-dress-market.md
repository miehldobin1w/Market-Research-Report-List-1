<p><ul><li>Wet Dress</li><li>Dry Dress</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diving dress market is categorized into two primary types: wet dress and dry dress. Wet dresses allow water to contact the diver's skin, providing thermal insulation through a layer of water trapped between the suit and skin, making them suitable for warmer waters. In contrast, dry dresses create a waterproof barrier that keeps the diver completely dry, utilizing air for insulation, thus enabling exploration in colder conditions. Each type serves specific environmental needs and diver preferences in underwater activities.</p></p>
