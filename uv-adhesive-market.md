<p><ul><li>Structural UV Adhesive</li><li>Sealant Adhesion UV Adhesive</li><li>Pressure Sensitive UV Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV adhesive market comprises various types, including structural UV adhesives, sealant adhesion UV adhesives, and pressure-sensitive UV adhesives. Structural UV adhesives are designed for high-strength bonding in demanding applications, providing durability and resilience. Sealant adhesion UV adhesives are tailored for sealing joints and gaps, ensuring water and air resistance. Pressure-sensitive UV adhesives offer immediate adhesion upon contact and are commonly used for labels and tapes, allowing for repositioning before curing, making them versatile for various industries.</p></p>
