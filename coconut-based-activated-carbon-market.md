<p><p>The coconut-based activated carbon market is segmented into three main types: Powdered Activated Carbon (PAC), Granular Activated Carbon (GAC), and Others. PAC is fine and used for applications requiring rapid adsorption, such as water treatment and air purification. GAC consists of larger granules, providing slower absorption, ideal for gas and liquid filtration. The "Others" category includes specialty forms and applications, catering to niche markets like medical and industrial uses, enhancing the versatility of coconut-based activated carbon solutions.</p></p>
