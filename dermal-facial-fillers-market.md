<p><p>The dermal facial fillers market is categorized into several types, including Hyaluronic Acid, Collagen, Artecoll, Calcium Hydroxylapatite (CaHA), and others. Hyaluronic Acid fillers are popular for their natural appearance and ability to retain moisture. Collagen fillers, derived from bovine sources, were once predominant but are less common now. Artecoll combines microspheres with collagen for a more permanent solution. CaHA fillers stimulate collagen production and last longer. Other fillers may include materials like polymethylmethacrylate (PMMA) and polylactic acid, each offering unique benefits.</p></p>
