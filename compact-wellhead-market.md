<p><p>The compact wellhead market includes two primary types: two-stage and three-stage wellheads. Two-stage wellheads facilitate oil and gas production by separating fluids in two distinct phases, enhancing efficiency and reducing space requirements. In contrast, three-stage wellheads further optimize the process by incorporating an additional separation phase, enabling more effective handling of mixed production streams and improving overall recovery rates. Both designs cater to varying operational needs and are essential in maximizing resource extraction in oil and gas fields.</p></p>
