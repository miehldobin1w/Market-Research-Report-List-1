<p><p>Transcranial Doppler devices are used to assess blood flow in the brain by utilizing ultrasound technology. The market is divided into two main types: M-mode display and B-mode display. M-mode display provides a one-dimensional view over time, capturing changes in blood flow velocity, which is useful for real-time monitoring. In contrast, B-mode display offers a two-dimensional image, allowing for better visualization of vascular structures and blood flow patterns, aiding in the diagnosis of various neurological conditions.</p></p>
