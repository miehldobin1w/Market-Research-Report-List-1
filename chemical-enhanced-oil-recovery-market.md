<p><ul><li>Polymer</li><li>Surfactant</li><li>ASP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemical Enhanced Oil Recovery (EOR) involves the use of various chemical agents to optimize oil extraction from reservoirs. The Polymer segment utilizes polymers to increase water viscosity, improving oil mobility. Surfactant EOR employs surfactants to reduce interfacial tension between oil and water, enhancing displacement efficiency. ASP EOR combines alkaline, surfactant, and polymer methods to maximize recovery by altering fluid properties and improving oil flow. Each type addresses specific reservoir characteristics to boost overall oil production effectively.</p></p>
