<p><p>The hemostasis products market is categorized into three main types: Topical Hemostasis, Infusible Hemostasis, and Advanced Hemostasis. Topical hemostasis products include gels, powders, and dressings applied directly to bleeding wounds. Infusible hemostasis products are solutions and agents administered intravenously to promote clotting in surgical or trauma settings. Advanced hemostasis products encompass biomaterials and innovative technologies designed to enhance blood coagulation, such as synthetic sealants and tissue adhesives, offering targeted solutions for various medical procedures.</p></p>
