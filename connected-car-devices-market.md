<p><strong>The Connected Car Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V)</li><li>Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2I)</li><li>Vehicle-to-Pedestrian(V2P)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Connected car devices enhance vehicular communication, categorized into three primary markets. Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) involves direct communication between vehicles to share information about speed, direction, and hazards, improving safety. Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2I) connects cars to road infrastructure like traffic signals and signs, optimizing traffic flow and reducing congestion. Vehicle-to-Pedestrian (V2P) focuses on safety interactions between vehicles and pedestrians, alerting drivers to nearby pedestrians and vice versa, thus promoting safer urban mobility. Together, these technologies aim to create smarter and safer transportation systems.</p></p>
