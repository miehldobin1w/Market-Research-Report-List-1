<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Hypertension Drug Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Calcium Channel Blockers</li><li>Diuretics</li><li>Beta Blockers Vasodilators</li><li>Angiotensin Converting Enzyme Inhibitors</li><li>Alpha Blockers</li><li>Angiotensin Receptor Blockers</li><li>Renin Inhibitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hypertension drug market comprises several key classes of medications. Calcium channel blockers relax blood vessels, while diuretics help reduce fluid overload. Beta blockers lower heart rate and pressure, and vasodilators directly widen blood vessels. Angiotensin-converting enzyme (ACE) inhibitors prevent the formation of a hormone that raises blood pressure. Alpha blockers relax blood vessel walls, and angiotensin receptor blockers (ARBs) block hormone activity. Lastly, renin inhibitors target the enzyme that initiates blood pressure regulation, offering diverse treatment options.</p></p>
