<p><p>The PET waste balers market comprises two main types: vertical and horizontal balers. Vertical PET waste balers are compact, suitable for smaller spaces, and ideal for low to moderate volumes of PET waste. They operate by compressing materials vertically, making them easier to handle. In contrast, horizontal PET waste balers are designed for high-capacity operations, efficiently processing large volumes of waste. They offer continuous feeding and are often used in industrial settings for bulk PET recycling, maximizing efficiency and space utilization.</p></p>
