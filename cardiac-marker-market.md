<p><p>The cardiac marker market comprises various types that aid in diagnosing heart conditions. Creatine kinase MB (CK-MB) helps assess myocardial damage, while troponins (cTnI and cTnT) are critical for detecting acute myocardial infarction. B-type natriuretic peptide (BNP) and its precursor NT-proBNP are used to evaluate heart failure severity. Myoglobin serves as an early indicator of myocardial injury. Additionally, markers like high-sensitivity C-reactive protein (hs-CRP) and D-dimer contribute to cardiovascular risk assessments and thromboembolism evaluations.</p></p>
