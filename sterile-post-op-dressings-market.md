<p><ul><li>Adhesive Foam Dressings</li><li>Hydrocolloids Dressings</li><li>Transparent Film Dressings</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Sterile Post-Op Dressings market encompasses various types designed for wound care and healing. Adhesive foam dressings provide cushioning and absorb excess exudate while maintaining a moist environment. Hydrocolloid dressings offer a moist wound environment, promoting healing and are ideal for low to moderate exudate. Transparent film dressings are used for protecting the skin and monitoring wound conditions without disrupting the healing process. Other types include alginates and non-adherent dressings, catering to specific wound needs and conditions in post-operative care.</p></p>
