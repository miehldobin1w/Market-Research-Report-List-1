<p><p>The artificial cervical disc market is categorized into three main types: metal-on-metal, metal-on-polymer, and others. Metal-on-metal discs utilize durable metal materials for both the endplates, providing strong structural support and minimal wear. Metal-on-polymer discs combine metal components with polymer inserts to enhance flexibility and reduce friction, promoting smoother movement. The "others" category includes various hybrid materials and designs that may integrate additional technologies or innovations, catering to diverse patient needs and preferences for cervical spine treatments.</p></p>
